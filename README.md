# Bookhub App
BookHub is your ultimate digital bookshelf, seamlessly blending functionality with personalized curation. Add books to your collection, categorize them into wishlists and favorites, track their prices, and rate your reads. Discover detailed insights and reviews, transforming your reading experience into an interactive, organized, and enjoyable journey. Dive into BookHub and elevate your literary adventures.

# Features
- Book Management: Add, categorize, track costs, and rate books.
- Dashboard: Overview of reading stats and personalized recommendations.
- Favourites: Quick access to wishlists and favorite books.
- Profile: Manage user preferences and reading history.
- About App: Insights into features and updates.

# Getting Started
- Download and Install: Get BookHub from your preferred app store and install it on your device.
- Add Books: Begin adding books to your library by searching or manually entering details.
- Organize: Use wishlists and categories to keep your collection sorted and easy to navigate.
- Explore: Check the Dashboard for reading stats and recommendations tailored just for you.
